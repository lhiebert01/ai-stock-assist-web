## Strategic spine (must survive any rewrite)

Every episode contains these 9 elements:

1. **Parable opening** — Alex tells a short story (200-300w) that mirrors the investing problem
2. **Beginner POV** — define every metric inline in plain English the first time it appears
3. **One specific objection demolished** — voiced by a cast member, demolished by Alex through demo
4. **One specific platform feature demoed live** — show what users actually see on screen
5. **Value math callout box** — ASCII format, every episode, recurring:
   ```
   ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  THE MATH                                            │
   │                                                      │
   │  Cost of one analysis:           ~$X                 │
   │  Cost of the mistake avoided:    ~$Y                 │
   │                                                      │
   │  Penny-wise. Pound-foolish.                          │
   └──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
   ```
6. **AI for Good close** — Alex says something memorable about wealth + wisdom + "stuck in your own ways"
7. **Standard CTA** — 3 free analyses, no credit card → aistockassist.com
8. **"One Ask Before You Go" engagement section** — REQUIRED, between Try This Yourself and Next Week. Three explicit asks:
   (a) Tap the ♥ heart at the top of the post (Substack algorithm signal)
   (b) Reply to a specific comment-prompt question (single, concrete, low-effort)
   (c) Forward to one specific person who needs this story
   Plus: "I read every comment and respond within 24 hours."
9. **Teaser for next episode** — one-line cliffhanger
